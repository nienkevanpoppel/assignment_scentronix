Whole-Grain Banana Bread
This one-bowl banana bread -- our 2018 Recipe of the Year -- uses the simplest ingredients, but is incredibly moist and flavorful. While the recipecalls for a 50/50 mix of flours (all-purpose and whole wheat), we often make the bread 100% whole wheat, and honestly? No one can tell, it's that good! And not only is this bread delicious -- it's versatile.
